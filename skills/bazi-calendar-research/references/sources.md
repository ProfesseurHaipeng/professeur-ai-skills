# Starting sources

- Chinese Text Project, [干支](https://ctext.org/dictionary.pl?char=%E5%B9%B2%E6%94%AF&if=en): terminology and sexagenary-cycle usage.
- Chinese Text Project, [史记·历书](https://ctext.org/shiji/li-shu1): historical calendar discourse.
- Use a named astronomical ephemeris or published calendar table for solar-term times; record version, timezone, and access date.

These sources establish textual and calendrical context; they do not validate predictive claims made by later divination schools.
