## Starting sources

- Perseus Digital Library, [Plato collection](https://www.perseus.tufts.edu/hopper/collection?collection=Perseus%3Acorpus%3Aperseus%2Cauthor%2CPlato): primary Greek texts and translations.
- Scaife Viewer, [pre-modern texts](https://scaife.perseus.org/): searchable text and aligned editions.
- National Endowment for the Humanities, [Perseus Digital Library](https://www.neh.gov/explore/perseus-digital-library): project provenance and educational context.

Record dialogue, section, language, translation, access date, and licence. Quote only short passages and prefer paraphrase with a direct source link.
