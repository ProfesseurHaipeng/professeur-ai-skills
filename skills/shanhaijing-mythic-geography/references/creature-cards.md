# Creature-card template

Each card should answer:

1. What is the original name and the shortest faithful description?
2. In which chapter and textual neighborhood does it occur?
3. What behavior, sound, body, diet, omen, or ritual relation is actually stated?
4. Which details are later illustration, translation choice, or modern fandom?
5. What competing readings exist for the name or body description?
6. What is the confidence label and source link?

Do not turn a creature into a biological species, medical ingredient, cultural stereotype, or “real animal” without an independent source. If generating artwork, include the original textual fragment in the alt text and label invented anatomy as adaptation.
