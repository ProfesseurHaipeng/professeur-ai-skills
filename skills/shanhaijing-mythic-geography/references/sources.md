## Starting sources

- Chinese Text Project, [山海经](https://ctext.org/datawiki.pl?if=en&res=603516): open text and chapter navigation.
- Academy of Contemporary China and World Studies, [Shan Hai Jing overview](https://www.china.org.cn/english/china_key_words/2024-08/29/content_117393756.html): concise historical and cultural context.
