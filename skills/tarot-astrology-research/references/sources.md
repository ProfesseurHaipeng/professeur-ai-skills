## Starting sources

- NASA Space Place, [What Are Constellations?](https://spaceplace.nasa.gov/constellations/en/): astronomy/astrology distinction and constellation basics.
- NASA Science, [Solar System Symbols](https://science.nasa.gov/resource/solar-system-symbols/): historical overlap between astronomical and astrological symbols.
- Prefer primary deck sources, museum catalogues, and dated scholarship for Tarot history; record edition, creator, date, and licence before reproducing imagery.

Do not present NASA's astronomy material as validation of astrological prediction. Record access dates and preserve the distinction between sky observation and symbolic interpretation.
