# Astronomy boundary

| Term | Technical meaning | Do not claim |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Constellation | Modern sky region defined by astronomical convention | that it is identical to a horoscope sign |
| Zodiac sign | A convention in a horoscope system | that it is a physical force |
| Ephemeris | A table/model of calculated celestial positions | that its output proves interpretation |
| Aspect | Angular relationship in a chosen coordinate frame | that it causes a life event |
| House | A chart partition dependent on time/location/system | that it is invariant across systems |

Explain precession and epoch when a user compares current constellations with tropical signs. Keep astronomy claims sourced to an observatory, space agency, or documented ephemeris.
