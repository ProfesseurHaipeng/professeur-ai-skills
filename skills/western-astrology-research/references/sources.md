# Starting sources

- NASA Space Place, [What Are Constellations?](https://spaceplace.nasa.gov/constellations/en/): astronomy versus astrology and constellation basics.
- NASA Science, [Skywatching FAQ](https://science.nasa.gov/skywatching/faq/): observable sky terminology.
- NASA Science, [Solar System Symbols](https://science.nasa.gov/resource/solar-system-symbols/): historical overlap of astronomical and astrological symbols.

Use an identified ephemeris for numerical positions and record its version and access date. NASA sources explain the astronomy boundary; they do not validate astrological prediction.
